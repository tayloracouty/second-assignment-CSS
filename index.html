Taylor Couty

Bio

Taylor Couty currently lives in New York, moving there a few years ago from Arizona. She studies Journalism and Design at Eugene Lang at The New School. She enjoys writing, photography and interior design. She has started about fifteen different journals in her life, and has yet to finish one. Before attending The New School, she worked in marketing at the Phoenix New Times Newspaper in Phoenix, Arizona.

Writing Examples

Taylor Couty has written pieces on the recent election, the controversy around female Trump Supporters, and the cost of university. Her most controversial piece yet was about the authenticity of politically active millennials.
